About

I’m a software engineer at ozon.ru in Moscow. Here I am working in a team as a Go backend engineer.

My background is in computer science, I did my undergrad at Saint-Petersburg State University. I did my thesis with JetBrains lab of AI tools for IntelliJ IDEA, and at the same time, I worked in a few part-time jobs-startups.

After the university, I wanted to develop highly distributed systems and joined a game company as a Python engineer. I learned a lot about large scale systems, the infrastructure around them, and played an important role in scaling core projects with increased business needs.

This work showed me that I want to grow professionally as an infrastructure engineer and I need to move to a bigger company. Also because more infrastructure systems are written in Go and not Python, I changed the stack and was recruited to ozon.ru. It’s an E-commerce online shop where we as a team do marketing mechanics for users. They are rapidly increase user’s loyalty and make a large profit.

In Ozon I learned a lot about large scale high availability architectures, and implemented some of the mechanics as a bunch of microservices connected with Kafka, using PostgreSQL as persistence storage and Redis as a cache.

Outside of work I like to dig into the open source and Linux systems (by the way I use Arch), active Vim user, write personal wikipedia for knowledge management and participate in tech online forums. To sum up, I’m looking for more tech or infrastructure domain and may be it’s your company!
